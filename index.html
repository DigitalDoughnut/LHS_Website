About David

David has a background in visual effects, having studied and later taught on the Digital Visual Effects MSc at the University of Kent. His colleagues and students have gone on to work on top Hollywood films including The Force Awakens, The Martian, Passengers and many more.

Since then, David has worked on many projects, but is currently concentrating on web development. He has a good working knowledge of HTML5, JavaScript, jQuery, PHP and MySQL, and is currently expanding his skillset into angular, React and node.js.
